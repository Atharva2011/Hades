Know About Us

We are a passionate team of innovators: Atharva Rai, Piyush Prajapati, Ritwik Mishra and Vaibhav Nikunj. Together, we are participating in Innovate@trix 25.0, where we have been challenged to transform the legendary game Hades into a groundbreaking culinary brand. Our team brings a perfect blend of creativity, strategy, and technical skills, united by a common vision: to reimagine immersive gaming experiences in a meaningful, ethical, and sustainable way. Through this project, we aim not just to design a unique food venture inspired by Hades' world, but also to address the challenges of consumerism and addiction that often lurk behind the gaming industry. We’re ready to level up the game — and your appetite!
